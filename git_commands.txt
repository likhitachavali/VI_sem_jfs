6. To remove a directory with content(non-empty): rmdir /s /q .<nameOfFolder>
7. To display hidden directories: dir /a:h
8. To rename a folder: rename <existing_folder> <new_folder>
9. To display content of a file: type <file_name>
10. To display folders: dir
11. To create a new text file: notepad  filename.txt
12. To forward the content of one file to another file: type 1file_name>2file_name
